# Dunder Mifflin Employee Handbook (MCP Resource)

Welcome to Dunder Mifflin.

## Core Principles
- Respect colleagues and customers.
- Keep communication clear, professional, and timely.
- Escalate policy violations through proper reporting channels.

## Workplace Conduct
- Discrimination, harassment, and retaliation are prohibited.
- Confidential information must not be disclosed outside approved channels.
- Follow manager direction and documented operational procedures.

## Attendance and Collaboration
- Maintain reliable attendance and punctuality.
- Coordinate schedule changes with your manager in advance.
- Collaborate with sales, warehouse, and support teams to meet customer commitments.
